The Raj Real Estate Story

Empowering Property Buyers Since 2022

At Raj Real Estate, we have been helping buyers and investors make confident property decisions since 2022. Under the leadership of our founder, Satish Kumar Sharma, we are committed to providing transparent guidance, verified properties, and a smooth buying experience. We focus on locations with strong growth potential, excellent connectivity, and opportunities for long-term value.

Every property we offer is carefully verified for clear titles, approvals from the relevant development authorities such as JDA, ADA and MMRDA where applicable, along with RERA registration wherever required. Our goal is to help our clients invest with confidence and choose properties that have the potential for future appreciation and lasting value.
